# Filenames Matter!
Due to the method that I am storing blog posts using git, some sort of quick identification is necessary.
Since its non-trivial for a filesystem to know whether a file is created or just renamed, its important that filenames dont change in this repo.

Because of this, a strict naming convention is used for article files.
It is formatted by the article's original title like so, **original-article-slug.md**.
